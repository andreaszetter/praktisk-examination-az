LoL Jungle clear times

How to add a champion:

Select a champion by searching in the field below(it has all champions before patch 14.20.1)

After selecting a champion, click the button to add it to the leaderboards.

Then you can track how fast your jungle clears are by typing them in the format XmXXs

There is also a textfield next to each champions Leaderboard that can be used to note things about your clears
